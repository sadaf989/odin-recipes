Lemon Cake

Decription

This homemade lemon cake is soft, buttery, and packed with fresh lemon flavor. It has a light, tender crumb and is topped with a sweet and tangy lemon glaze, making it perfect for dessert or enjoying with tea or coffee.

This recipe is simple to prepare using basic ingredients and creates a moist, flavorful cake with a refreshing citrus taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup/200g unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda

For the Lemon Glaze

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the granulated sugar and lemon zest. Mix for about a minute until the sugar becomes fragrant.
  3. Add the eggs, melted and cooled butter, and lemon juice. Whisk until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour and baking soda.
  5. Gradually fold the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients, mixing just until a smooth batter forms. Avoid overmixing.
  6. Pour the batter into your prepared baking pan or dish and spread it evenly.
  7. Bake for 18–20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the cake to cool completely before adding the glaze.

For the lemon glaze

  1. In a small bowl, combine the powdered sugar and lemon juice.
  2. Whisk until the glaze is smooth and creamy.
  3. Once the cake has cooled, drizzle the glaze evenly over the top. Let it set for a few minutes before serving.
